My Favourite Movies Of All Time

Shawshank Redemption

"Because it shows how hope can survive even in the darkest, most hopeless places."
It’s not just a prison story — it’s about freedom, friendship, and quiet strength.

The Godfather

"Because it shows how power, family, and loyalty can build — and destroy — an empire."
It’s not just a crime film; it’s a mirror of ambition, silence, and control.

The Dark Knight

"Because the Joker makes you question the line between chaos and order."
A superhero movie that’s really about society, fear, and the choices we make.

Titanic

"It’s not just a love story — it’s about fate, sacrifice, and what lasts forever."
The visuals and emotions still hold up after all these years.

3 Idiots

"Because it challenged everything we’re told about success and education."
It made me laugh, cry, and think — all at the same time.

The Founder

"Because it shows how vision and ruthlessness can change the world."
It’s uncomfortable, honest, and fascinating.

Jobs

"Because it shows how a flawed man changed the way we live."
It’s messy, brilliant, and driven — just like the man.

Wolf Of The Wallstreet

"Because it shows the dangerous seduction of money and power."
Wild, hilarious, and disturbingly real.

The Notebook

"Because it reminds me that true love doesn’t fade — even when memories do."
It’s raw, emotional, and timeless.

Social Network

"Because it’s not just about Facebook — it’s about ambition, betrayal, and legacy."
Sharp writing, great music, and even better acting.
